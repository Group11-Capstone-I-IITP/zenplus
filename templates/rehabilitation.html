Ice Bath Recovery

Step-by-Step Guide for Beginners

Step-by-step Instructions

  • 1. Fill tub with cold water and add ice
  • 2. Check temperature (10–15°C)
  • 3. Enter slowly to avoid shock
  • 4. Focus on breathing
  • 5. Stay for 5–15 minutes
  • 6. Exit carefully
  • 7. Warm up naturally
