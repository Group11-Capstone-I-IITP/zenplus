Happiness & Mindfulness

Step-by-Step Guide for Beginners

What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness means paying attention to the present moment. It helps you stay calm and avoid stress by focusing on what is happening now.

Daily Steps

  • Wake up and take deep breaths
  • Stay focused on what you are doing
  • Don't think too much about past or future
  • Be thankful for small things
  • Take short breaks
  • Meditate for few minutes

Benefits

Less stress
Better focus
Good sleep
More happiness

Simple Happiness Tips

Spend time with family
Exercise daily
Stay positive
Enjoy small moments
