← 🧘 Meditation Beginner's Guide

1. Comfortable Posture

  • Sit comfortably without falling asleep
  • Keep your back straight
  • Hands on lap or knees
  • Close eyes or look down

2. Focus on Breathing

  • Take a deep breath in
  • Hold for a moment
  • Breathe out slowly
  • Repeat and relax

3. Handle Distractions

If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to breathing.

4. Body Relaxation

  • Feet → Feel grounded
  • Legs → Relax muscles
  • Stomach → Soften
  • Heart → Feel calm
  • Shoulders → Drop tension
  • Face → Relax completely

5. Energy Flow

Imagine your breath moving through your entire body.

6. No Judgment

Meditation is practice. Everything is okay.

7. Finish Slowly

  • Take 3 deep breaths
  • Feel energy from toes to head
  • Slowly open your eyes
