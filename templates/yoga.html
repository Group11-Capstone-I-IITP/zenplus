10-Minute Morning Yoga

Back to Meditation

This gentle 10-minute yoga routine helps you start your day with energy, focus, and calmness. No equipment needed—just your body and breath.

Grounding & Warm-up
  • Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat
  • Relax shoulders, palms facing up
  • Pull right knee to chest, extend left leg
  • Rotate ankle and stretch toes
  • Twist gently to the left
  • Repeat on other side
Bridge & Leg Stretch
  • Lift hips into Bridge Pose
  • Squeeze glutes and thighs
  • Slowly lower down
  • Cross ankle for Reclined Pigeon stretch
  • Stretch hamstring upward
  • Repeat both sides
Flow Movements
  • Move into Downward Dog
  • Shift into plank
  • Lower to belly
  • Lift chest in Cobra Pose
  • Rest in Child’s Pose
Seated Stretches
  • Neck stretch side to side
  • Gentle seated twist
  • Open chest and lift heart
  • Bring hands together and breathe
  • Close with one “Om”
